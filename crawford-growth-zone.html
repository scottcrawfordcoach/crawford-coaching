Email & Privacy

Why we ask, and how we look after your data

The Growth Zone runs on trust. The exercises only work if you're honest, and you can only be honest if you know the answers aren't going to end up somewhere you don't want them. Here's the deal, in plain language.

Why we ask for email

  • Your results are saved to your account. Sign in any time, on any device, and find what you've done. Re-run an exercise months later and see how your wheel, your values, or your motivation profile have shifted.
  • The full Growth Zone connects the dots. Cross-exercise reporting needs to know all your results belong to the same person. Email is what makes that possible.
  • It's also how we stay in touch. If you opt in, you'll receive occasional newsletters, new exercise announcements, and coaching offers. If you don't opt in, you'll only hear from us about your own account.

How we look after your information

  • Privacy-first by design. We use Google Analytics to understand site engagement in aggregate. Beyond that we don't run advertising trackers, sell visitor data, or share it with marketers.
  • Canadian servers. Your results and account data are stored on infrastructure based in Canada and governed by Canadian privacy law.
  • Ephemeral AI calls, enterprise endpoints. When an AI report is generated, your data is sent through a single-use commercial API call to a model provider whose contract prohibits retention or training on your data. We do not use public consumer interfaces (the kind you'd log into yourself) for this.
  • Never shared, never sold. Your information is not passed to third parties for marketing, data brokerage, or any purpose other than running the Growth Zone.
  • No advertising. The Growth Zone shows no third-party ads. The only marketing emails you'll receive are the ones you specifically opt into, and you can unsubscribe at any time.
  • You're in control of your data. At any time you can export everything we hold about you, or delete your account and wipe it. No friction, no support ticket required.
  • Cancellation is your choice, twice over. When you cancel a Growth Zone subscription, we'll ask whether you want to keep your results in case you return, or delete everything along with your account. Both options are fully supported.
Read the full privacy policy for the complete detail on cookies, analytics, and your rights. Questions or specific requests about your data? Get in touch directly.
