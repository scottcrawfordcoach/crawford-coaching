A note before you start

These aren't tests. They're prompts to look at yourself, briefly and on purpose.

Quick snapshots, not formal psychological assessments. They're designed to surface something worth talking about — with yourself, a coach, a counsellor, or a friend who's earned the conversation. The point isn't a score. The point is the line of thinking the score nudges you into.

If anything that comes up here feels heavier than a prompt should, that's worth knowing too. A credentialed coach or counsellor can help you unpack what showed up and work towards meaningful change.
