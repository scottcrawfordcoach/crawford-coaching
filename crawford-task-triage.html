Growth Zone

Task
Triage

When everything feels equally urgent, nothing gets done. This tool helps you step back, see the shape of what is on your plate, and figure out where to start.

You will list the tasks, projects, or decisions that are competing for your attention right now. Then you will rate how complete each one is and rank them across a few key dimensions. The result is a clear, personalised recommendation for where to focus first.

It does not matter whether your list includes small chores or large projects. If something on your list feels too big to act on, you can always run this exercise again with just the sub-tasks of that one item to break it down further.

Interactive · about 5 minutes

Your first name (for your summary)

What is on your plate?

List between 3 and 10 tasks, projects, decisions, or chores that are competing for your time and attention right now.

You can remove items by hovering and clicking the × on the right.

Completion Wheel

Click a ring in each sector to rate how complete that task is, from 0 (centre) to 10 (outer edge). You can adjust any sector until you are happy with the picture.

Rank Your Tasks

For each dimension, arrange your tasks from most to least. Click a task name, then click a slot to place it.

What You're Looking At

Pulling together the picture from your rankings.

Thinking through your priorities...

If any task on your list still feels too large, run this exercise again with just the sub-tasks of that item to break it down further.

Turn this into a plan

You have the picture. The next step is working out what you are actually going to do. Enter your email to receive a personalised coaching worksheet with guided questions for your top priorities: what does done look like, what might get in the way, when will you start. No advice, just the right questions to help you think it through.

How your data is used

Your exercise results are stored securely on Canadian servers. They are linked to your email address so that future exercise reports can reference your prior results and offer more connected insight.

The AI-generated report is produced through a single, ephemeral API call to a language model. No personal identifiers beyond your first name are included in the request. The AI provider does not store your data or use it for training.

Your information is never shared, sold, or used for any purpose other than delivering your reports and, if you opt in, occasional emails from Crawford Coaching. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Questions? Reach out directly.
