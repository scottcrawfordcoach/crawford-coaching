Product Structure

Four products live, one parked. Each product has its own billing model, but they connect through clear bridging rules: active services bundle access, and discounts only ever apply once.

Service · Premium

1:1 Coaching

Per-package or ongoing

The core coaching offer. Sporadic by nature — managed by relationship status rather than rigid billing cycles.

Bundles Full Growth Zone (excl. WHOLE content) while marked active

Course · 16 Weeks

WHOLE

Course fee

Structured 16-week programme. Growth Zone exercises form integral parts of the coursework.

Bundles + Trail Full Growth Zone during course · 30% alumni discount on monthly GZ, claimable once at course end

Membership · Recurring

Synergize Fitness

Monthly

Gym membership product. Members receive an online tools area and the option to add Growth Zone at a discount.

Bundles + Bridge Synergize Members Area included · 30% off monthly GZ while billing active

Growth Zone

Self-discovery and tools platform. Standalone subscription with full access to all psychology-based exercises, AI reports, and advanced fitness tooling.

Discount eligibility 30% off monthly via active Synergize OR claimed WHOLE alumni status

Override Rule Active service status always wins over discount calculations. Discounts never stack — highest single discount applies. Annual subscriptions are always full price; discounts apply to monthly only.

Authentication & Access Resolution

Magic-link authentication followed by sequential status checks. The first matching status determines access; later checks are skipped. This is the canonical decision order for the role resolver.

1

First Check

Coaching status = active?

→
Full Growth Zone (excluding WHOLE content) Bundled in coaching fee · status managed manually
2

Second Check

WHOLE status = active?

→
Full Growth Zone Bundled in course fee · 16-week duration
3

Third Check

Growth Zone subscription active?

→
Full Growth Zone Stripe is source of truth for subscription state
4

Fourth Check

Synergize status = active?

→
Synergize Members Area + Growth Zone if subscribed (30% off) Discount applies only while Synergize billing is active
5

Fifth Check

WHOLE alumni discount claimed and GZ subscription active?

→
Full Growth Zone at 30% rate Permanent for that subscription · lapses if subscription ends
6

Sixth Check

Signed in, no paid or bundled status?

→
Growth Zone free tier Core Values, Strengths, Feelings Guide + basic timer tools
7

Default · No account

Not signed in

→
Free /timer only Clock, single countdown, Quick EMOM · invitation to Growth Zone for full timer & workout tools
